Welcome to iSafe

Our Mission:

iSafe allows women to report incidents anonymously via SMS to a twitter account @isafety4all. The incidents can range from cat calling to physical abuse, and the type of incident and level of threat is specified through a hashtag. If one user has reported multiple incidents, or has stated a high threat level, our tool will set up a connection with an NGO close to them by initiating a DM message between both parties. For neighborhood with many reported incidents, iSafe will create a petition on change.org, tweeting the details to gain backers and increase awareness of the issue.
